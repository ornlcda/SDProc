Workshop Program and Proceedings

The 1st Workshop on Scholarly Document Processing will be held online in conjunction with the 2020 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP 2020). The workshop will be held as a full-day event on November 19, 2020 (after the main conference).
