Previous Workshops

Our workshop is a continuation of several previous ones on similar topics.

We have been organising the Bibliometric-enhanced Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing for Digital Libraries (BIRNDL) since 2016: 1st at JCDL '16, 2nd, 3rd and 4th at SIGIR.

The Workshop on Mining Scientific Publications (WOSP) has run every year between 2012 and 2018 alongside JCDL, and later LREC.

The Workshop on Scholarly Web Mining at the International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining (WSDM) focused on mining and analysing scholarly data on web and social media.

In previous years, the Text Analytics Conference (TAC) at NIST hosted a series of workshops addressing various aspects of text summarization. Most recently, its 2014 Biomedical Summarization track hosted a shared task comparing Biomedicine scientific articles and their citations to other articles.
